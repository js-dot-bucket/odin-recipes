Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken is a classic Indian dish known for its rich, creamy, and mildly spiced tomato-based gravy with tender chicken pieces cooked in butter and cream. This flavorful recipe combines marinated chicken with a luscious gravy that balances spice, tanginess, and sweetness, making it a beloved comfort food often enjoyed with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken pieces (thigh or breast)
  • Yogurt (for marination)
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Tomatoes (pureed or fresh tomato puree)
  • Butter and cream (for the rich, creamy gravy)

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger garlic paste, red chili powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt; let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook or grill the marinated chicken until tender and slightly charred.
  3. Heat butter in a pan, saut√© whole spices and ginger garlic paste, then add pureed tomatoes and cook until oil separates.
  4. Add spices like garam masala, kasuri methi, and cream; simmer the gravy until thick and creamy.
  5. Combine cooked chicken with the gravy, simmer together for 5-10 minutes, garnish with fresh cream and coriander, and serve hot.
