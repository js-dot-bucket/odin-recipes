Pizza

Description

Pizza is a versatile and delicious dish that typically features a crisp, chewy crust topped with tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of toppings. Making pizza at home allows you to customize the flavors and ingredients to your liking, whether you prefer classic Margherita, veggie-loaded, or meat-topped varieties. The basic components include dough, sauce, cheese, and toppings, all baked to perfection for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (made with flour, yeast, warm water, olive oil, salt, and sugar)
  • Tomato/pizza sauce (pureed tomatoes, garlic, herbs like oregano and basil)
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded or fresh)
  • Olive oil (for dough and drizzling)
  • Toppings (such as sliced bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, pepperoni, or fresh basil)

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough by mixing flour, yeast, warm water, olive oil, salt, and sugar; let it rise until it doubles in size.
  2. Roll out the dough into a circular shape on a floured surface and place it on a baking tray or pizza stone dusted with cornmeal.
  3. Spread an even layer of tomato sauce over the rolled dough.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese followed by your choice of toppings.
  5. Bake the pizza in a preheated oven at around 450°F (230°C) for 10–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly and melted.
